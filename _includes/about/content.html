Phil or Chapavich Temnitikul is an award winning Thai film score & multimedia music composer and experimental artist (aka. Phil_wc)

Mainly, Phil composes music for films, games, TV and stage show. Moreover, He is a multi-genres composer who makes music in many genres such as Electronic, EDM, Trance, Electro House, Experimental, Noise, and Ambient.

Biography

Chapavich Temnitikul or Phil began studying piano at the age of 6. Phil was interested in music composition. He graduated in music composition from Rangsit University and Film Scoring from UCLA. Phil started composing for film when he was 19. He has done great deals of films and media in Thailand. His works were shown in many film festivals around the world. In 2010, he got an award in film scoring competition that persuaded him to be a film composer. Moreover, he got a scolarship to do a film scoring workshop at FilmSoundHamburg, Germany in 2012 with Jan A. P. Kaczmarek.

Not only film music, but Phil is also working on experimental music on his own. He does live performance and produce ambient and experimental music. He has composed and listened to music in various styles such as Electronic Dance Music, experimental, progressive, and avant-garde music. Because of wide-range of interested, he work for both commercial and arts.
